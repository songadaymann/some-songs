(1) Go to http://somesongs.org/ (2) Click "Log In With Facbeook" in the right side of the top nav bar (3) Add the app (4) Enter additional required registration info (email, location) (5) Click "My Info" in the top nav bar (6) Click the "Enable Pulbishing to Timeline" button (7) Grant the app permission to publish to Timeline (8) Once back on the site click "Songs" in the top nav bar (9) Click the good/okay/bad rating button for a song, this should publish your action to your Timeline
